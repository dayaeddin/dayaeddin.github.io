            <h2 class="section-title">📜 Biography</h2>
            <p>I am a <strong>Ph.D. graduate in Artificial Intelligence and Natural Language Processing</strong> (February 2025) from the <strong>University of Abbas Laghrour, Algeria</strong>, with a research focus on <strong>Neural Machine Translation</strong>, <strong>Arabic NLP</strong>, and <strong>low-resource language processing</strong>. I maintain active collaborations with international research institutions, including a research internship at the <strong>University of Malta</strong> (2023).</p>
